Get Ready to Sort Things Out (literally), Human Bean! 🚮

Did you know that the average person has the opportunity to recycle more than 25,000 cans in his or her lifetime? That recycling a single aluminum can saves enough energy to power a television for three whole hours?

At SORT FOR EARTH, we know that you care about the planet and so do we. That's why we made a nifty-as-heck machine learning tool to help you sort your trash with more ease and accuracy. Follow two easy steps to find out if your trash is recycling, compost, garbage, or a non-compostable material. Happy sorting, fellow earthlings!
